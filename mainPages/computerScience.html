Computer Science

Computer science is the study of computers, their design, the way they process information, and how they can be used to solve problems. It is the theory, design, and application of computers. Computer science is a large field that draws its foundations from math and engineering, and it involves computer hardware, software, and networks. Computer science is important because we live in a world that relies heavily on technology, and computers can help us solve problems and make our lives easier, more fun, and safer.
